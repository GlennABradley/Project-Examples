Survival Rate by Different Variables

If comparing between the column named Sex, there were far more male passengers than female that didnt survive.

According to the description, the Pclass column is corresponding to the ticket classes, which is a proxy for social classes. As we can see from the chart, a lot of men died from the 2nd and 3rd clas and while the majority of women survived, the 3rd class has a lot of female victims.

Summary

After looking over our data in the Titanic dataset, we can come away with a few observations. Below, the data is grouped by Sex and ticket class, also gives up the count for each group. We can assume from the data that the odds of survival drastically improved with ticket class, women who were in 1st or 2nd class had the highest avgerage of survival. The same cant be said for males, who no matter what class you were in had lower odds of survival.
